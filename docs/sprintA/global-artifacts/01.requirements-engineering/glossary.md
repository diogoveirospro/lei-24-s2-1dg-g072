# Glossary

**Terms, Expressions and Acronyms (TEA) must be organized alphabetically.**

| **_TEA_** (EN)              | **_Description_** (EN)                                                                                                                                                       |                                       
|:----------------------------|:-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Human Resources Manager** | Person responsible for managing the human resources and defines teams based on the needs of ongoing projects and the skills of employees.                                    |
| **GRH**                     | Acronym for _Human Resources Manager_.                                                                                                                                       |
| **Vehicle Park Manager**    | Person responsible for managing the machine park, equipment and vehicles, ensuring their good condition and assigning them to tasks to perform.                              |
| **GPV**                     | Acronym for _Vehicle Park Manager_                                                                                                                                           |
| **Collaborator**            | A person who is an employee of the organisation and carries out tasks related to the design, construction and/or maintenance of green areas, according to their competences. |
| **Green Spaces Manager**    | Person responsible for the management of green spaces in charge of the organisation.                                                                                         |
| **GEV**                     | Acronym for _Green Spaces Manager_.                                                                                                                                          |
| **Green Spaces User**       | Person who use the green spaces managed by the organisation and who can, through the Portal, make comments or report faults in the parks and gardens.                        |
